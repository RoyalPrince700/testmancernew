Introduction to English Language Learning

Course Overview

Basic Sentence Structure

English sentences follow a Subject-Verb-Object (SVO) pattern:

Subject + Verb + Object
Example: I (subject) eat (verb) apples (object).

Common Sentence Types:

  • Declarative: Makes a statement - "I like English."
  • Interrogative: Asks a question - "Do you like English?"
  • Imperative: Gives a command - "Learn English!"
  • Exclamatory: Shows strong emotion - "English is amazing!"

Essential Vocabulary

Greetings and Basic Expressions:

  • Hello / Hi - Greeting
  • Goodbye / Bye - Farewell
  • Thank you / Thanks - Gratitude
  • Please - Politeness
  • Excuse me - To get attention
  • I'm sorry - Apology

Question Words:

  • What - Used for things
  • Where - Used for places
  • When - Used for time
  • Why - Used for reasons
  • How - Used for methods
  • Who - Used for people
